About Our Shop

Founded with a passion for flour, sugar, and creativity, Sweet Treats has been serving the community since 2018. We specialize in custom wedding cakes, artisanal birthday treats, and daily fresh-baked pastries made from 100% organic, locally sourced ingredients.

Our mission is simple: to combine stunning visual designs with unforgettable flavors.
